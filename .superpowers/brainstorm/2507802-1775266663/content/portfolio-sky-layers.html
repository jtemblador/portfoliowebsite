What should the portfolio background sky show?

The full viewer has 10+ render layers. Which ones should be visible behind your portfolio content?

1

Stars (with twinkle)

15,598 real stars with color and brightness. The core visual — always on.

2

Milky Way glow

Subtle diffuse band across the sky. Adds depth without competing with text.

3

Planets + Sun + Moon

Bright orange/yellow dots. Noticeable but not distracting.

4

Constellation lines

Thin white lines connecting stars. Could be too busy behind text.

5

Labels (constellations, DSOs, planets)

Text labels in the sky. Would definitely compete with portfolio text.

6

Twilight glow

Warm horizon glow when Sun is below horizon. Nice ambient touch.

7

Horizon line + Cardinals

White horizon line with N/S/E/W markers. Adds spatial grounding.

Click all the layers you'd want visible in portfolio mode. I'd suggest: stars + Milky Way + planets + twilight (1, 2, 3, 6) — enough to feel alive without competing with your content.
